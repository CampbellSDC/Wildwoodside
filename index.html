A little about me...

I moved to Atlanta in 2006 to attend graduate school at the University of Georgia. After falling in love with the "winters" in the South, we called Atlanta home. Currently, I am working for a local furniture store and building my own pieces in my spare time. I am also learning web development and hope to transition into tech soon and let woodworking be a hobby for me.


Extremely blessed to have two amazing daughters of my own, and two amazing bonus daughters, along with a very supportive, and very patient (and equally amazing), wife.
